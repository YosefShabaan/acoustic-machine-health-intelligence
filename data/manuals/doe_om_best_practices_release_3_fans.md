This tracked file is a compact AMHI RAG source note derived from an official
DOE/FEMP operations and maintenance guide. It is not a replacement for the full
guide and must be used only as retrieval evidence for cautious
inspection-oriented communication.

## DOE-OM-R3-MAINTENANCE-PROGRAMS

Release 3.0 distinguishes reactive, preventive, predictive, and reliability
centered maintenance concepts. AMHI should present acoustic evidence as an input
to inspection and condition review, not as proof of a fault. Retrieved guidance
can support scheduling inspections, trending observations, and using existing
maintenance processes.

## DOE-OM-R3-PREDICTIVE-MAINTENANCE

The guide describes predictive maintenance as using quantified equipment
condition to guide maintenance tasks. AMHI acoustic evidence is one condition
monitoring input and must be kept separate from maintenance confirmation. The
system may recommend review by qualified maintenance personnel and comparison
with other available measurements when retrieved guidance supports that wording.

## DOE-OM-R3-AIR-HANDLING-CHECKLIST

The guide includes an air handling checklist covering visual inspection, filter
condition, system integrity, damper operation, filter rack integrity, pressure
measurement components, and coil cleanliness. For a fan acoustic event, AMHI may
retrieve this section to support checking related air-handling context such as
filters, dampers, duct leakage, access doors, and airflow-related constraints.

## DOE-OM-R3-FAN-CHECKLIST-VISUAL

The fan checklist includes overall visual inspection to confirm equipment
operation and safety systems, plus system use and sequencing checks. AMHI may
recommend a visual inspection and verification that unnecessary equipment is not
running or sequenced incorrectly when the retrieved source supports it.

## DOE-OM-R3-FAN-CHECKLIST-MECHANICAL

The fan checklist includes observing belts for proper tension and alignment,
inspecting pulley wheels, cleaning and lubricating where required, inspecting
dampers, checking actuator and linkage operation, and verifying fan blade
rotation and cleaning needs. AMHI may recommend these inspections as source
grounded next actions only when retrieval returns this chunk.

## DOE-OM-R3-FAN-CHECKLIST-DUCT-ELECTRICAL

The fan checklist includes checking wiring for tight electrical connections,
inspecting ductwork, refastening loose connections, repairing leaks, checking
filters, and inspecting duct insulation. AMHI may recommend these broad
inspection checks but must not state that acoustic evidence confirms an
electrical, duct, or mechanical failure.

## Limits For AMHI Use

- Use as approved public source evidence for facility fan and air-handling
  inspection context.
- Do not use as a production work order generator without site procedures.
- Do not use to confirm a component-level failure.
- Do not claim RUL, time to failure, confidence, or probability.
- Do not claim the AMHI pipeline has validated maintenance outcomes.
